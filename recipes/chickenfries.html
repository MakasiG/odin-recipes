Chicken Strips and Fries Recipe

Description

This is one of the simplest recipes to make. Whether you're tired from work, school or you're just feeling too lazy to cook, this is a great option. It is simple and has very little preparation time. On top of that, it is still delicious and won't dissapoint. You can also make this recipe if you're feeling hungry but are in a rush to go somewhere. The serving for the recipe below is for one individual. You can increase the amount of ingredients if you need to serve more than one.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 kg of potatoes
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 teaspon salt
  • 1/2 teaspon black pepper

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes thinly. Follow suit with the bellpapers and chicken breast. Place each in a separate bowl.
  2. Add cooking oil into a frying pan. Heat for about 3 minutes (medium heat)
  3. Add the chicken and fries at once and deep fry
  4. Let the two deep fry for about seven minutes on medium heat and add the bell peppers
  5. Fry for three minutes then turn off the heat
  6. Transfer to plate, and add salt and paper. The meal is ready
